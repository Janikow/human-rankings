About Reaction Time

Average human reaction time is 200–250ms. Elite gamers often hit 150–180ms. Pro athletes can reach 120ms. Regular training can improve your baseline significantly.

About CPS (Clicks Per Second)

Average players click 5–8 CPS. Jitter clicking can reach 14–16 CPS. Butterfly clicking can exceed 20 CPS. Fast clicking is crucial in PvP games like Minecraft.
